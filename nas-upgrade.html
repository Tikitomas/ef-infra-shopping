DMD catch (chassis): Original plan had 2× TX401. The TX401 is full-height (98.2mm) and physically will not fit the TS-855eU 2U rackmount (68.9mm PCIe clearance). QNAP QXG cards are 67mm by design. TX401 stays for Socrates (desktop tower).
DMD catch (RAM compat): OWC + NEMIX SODIMMs removed 2026-05-16 — neither is on QNAP's TS-855eU memory compatibility list (verified against `C:\Users\Thomas\Downloads\Nas Expandability\TS-855eU_Memory.csv`). Only 4 modules are approved: 16GB / 32GB × ECC / non-ECC, all QNAP-branded RAM-xxGDR4(EC)K0-SO-3200.
DMD catch (NIC compat): Scorptec "QXG-10G2T" (SKU 108803) removed 2026-05-16 — not explicitly on the TS-855eU expansion card list. On-list dual-port variants are QXG-10G2TB, QXG-10G2T-X710, and QXG-10G2T-107 only. QXG-10G1T (Mwave) and QXG-10G2TB (Scorptec) are the two safe buys.
